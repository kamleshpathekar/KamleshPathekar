E-Sabha

Used the Django Web Framework to implement an E-Sabha portal.

About

E-sabha is a digital tool that allows users to quickly create and share content with the public. Social media encompasses a wide range of websites and apps. Some, like Twitter, specialize in sharing links and short written messages. Others, like Instagram and Facebook, are built to optimize the sharing of photos and videos.

Anyone with internet access can sign up on a E-Sabha account. They can use that account to share whatever content they choose to, and the content they share reaches anyone who visits their page or profile.
